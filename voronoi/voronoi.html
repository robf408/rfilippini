Voronoi Diagram

DrawVoronoi

The Voronoi diagram of a set of points in the plane (referred to as sites) simply consists of cells and cell boundaries where the cell corresponding to a site P consists of all points whose distance to P is less than or equal to the distance to any other site. If P and Q are sites, and if the corresponding cells share an edge, then P and Q are connected in the Delaunay Triangulation.

This is a pure ActionScript implementation of Fortune's sweep algorithm to compute the Voronoi Diagram of a set of points and the associated Delaunay Triangulation.

Click on stage to create new site. Sites can be dragged and are eliminated if dragged off the stage.
